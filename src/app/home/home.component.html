Tour:

Definition:

"Tour" refers to a journey for pleasure, education, or business, often involving multiple destinations and a structured itinerary, while "travel" is the broader concept of moving from one place to another, encompassing various purposes and levels of planning.


Tour and Travel Information


Definition: A journey for pleasure, education, or business that involves a series of stops and often ends at the starting point.

Characteristics: A tour typically involves a pre-planned itinerary. It may include guided activities and excursions and is often organized by a tour operator or company.

Examples: Some common types of tours include a sports tour, an educational tour, and a music tour.

More Examples: A guided city tour, a bus tour, a music band's world tour, a river cruise, and an expedition cruise.
